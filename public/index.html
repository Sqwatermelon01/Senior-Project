How to play: Click the start button to reveal the daily riddle. The riddle has a single-word answer, and you have three attempts to solve the riddle (do not include 'a', 'an', or 'the' in your answer).

Scoring: Once the start button is clicked, a timer will start. Your score is determined by how quickly you solve the riddle and the number of attempts you have left. (The score will only be added if you are signed in.)

The daily leaderboard: The daily leaderboard will reset every day at 8:00AM (CST).

The monthly leaderboard: The monthly leaderboard will reset on the 1st of every month. Every day you solve a riddle, your score accumulates until it is reset.
